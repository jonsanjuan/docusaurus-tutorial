Manage Company

This Admin module lets you control what Companies can be assigned to Users when setting up their profiles.

Companies

When creating a User's Profile, you must assign them a Company. This can then be used for limiting a User's access to other Users. See Manage Roles for more info.

Creating a new Company

Click the "Create Company" button. Enter a name for it, then click the to save it.

Editing an existing Company

Click the to change the name of the Company, then click the when you're done.

Deleting a Company

Click the icon of a Company to delete it.
